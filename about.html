ABOUT US

Quanomic's story began in 2008, fueled by a shared frustration among our founders â€“ all experienced water resource professionals. We have witnessed the challenges plaguing irrigation project planning and execution, particularly during the crucial study and design phase.

While advancements in software technology revolutionised design processes in sectors like roads and buildings, irrigation development remained largely unchanged for decades. We saw this as a critical disadvantage for the sector and a challenge we were determined to overcome.

Our journey began with a groundbreaking innovation: a successful integration of a powerful Matlab-based API into the widely used AutoCAD environment. This merged the strengths of cutting-edge numerical computation with user-friendly graphics, empowering professionals with a new design toolset.

Continuous Improvement and Growth:

Through valuable feedback from professionals and real-world project applications, iCAD underwent rapid development. Not only were its algorithms validated for accuracy and completeness, but the software also received industry approval for mainstream use.

Today and Beyond:

Quanomic now boasts a robust software suite with over 21 production-level design modules within iCAD, alongside the standalone CanalNETWORK product. We are actively expanding our network of partners, including regional design enterprises, consulting firms, government ministries, and universities, with the shared goal of revolutionising the irrigation sector throughout Africa.
